Meet Raman Bhandari

"Making digital services simple for every Nepali"

I started helping people with digital services long before I came back to Nepal — even while I was working in Qatar at Al Baker Group and Al Baironi Trading, people would reach out and I would find a way to help. That's when I realized this was more than just a side thing. After 8+ years abroad, I came back to Kathmandu and built Digital Hub Online Nepal properly — one place where any Nepali, whether they're home or working overseas, can get things done without the stress, confusion, or middlemen. Since then I've helped 5,000+ clients with everything from flight bookings and Shram approvals to SSF registration, driving licenses, and professional CVs. My goal has always been simple — make digital services fast, affordable, and trustworthy for every Nepali. This is just the beginning. — Raman Bhandari
